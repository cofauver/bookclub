List your favorites

Sign up for an account and add your favorite books. We're talking about those books that you would love to discuss with others. We'll show you what other Matchbookers are reading too!

Let us make the match

We'll keep a lookout for others listing the same books as you. When there are at least three readers for a single book, we'll contact all of you and let you know there's a critical mass.

Schedule a meeting

Find out how far along other readers are or if they're ready to chat. Set up a time to video chat about the books you love.
